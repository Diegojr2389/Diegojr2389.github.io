What is it?

FormFit is a mobile application designed for anyone who is interested in improving their workout form and technique. It utilizes machine learning and pose detection technology to analyze your body movements in real time and provides instant feedback on your form. The app aims to enhance workout effectiveness, reduce the risk of injury, and help you achieve your fitness goals more efficiently.

Apart from providing real-time feedback, it offers a chatbot for additional support and guidance. It can also answer questions on different weightlifting topics including but not limited to: what exercises are best for what muscle groups, what workout split is best for your goals, and what exercises can be done if you have limited equipment.

Why Use It

FormFit provides real-time feedback to help you correct your form and technique, leading to more effective workouts and a reduced risk of injury. It doesn't matter if you are a beginner or an experienced athlete, FormFit can help you improve your performance and reduce the risk of injury through form-correcting guidance.

With its chatbot feature, you can get personalized advice and support whenever you need it. You might want to get questions answered ASAP, or might just want some motivational feedback! Well, FormFit is the right app for you!

How To Use It

To use FormFit, simply download the app on your mobile device, set up your profile, and navigate through the dfferent tabs. You can either go straight to the chatbot tab and ask it questions or you can jump into the workouts tab which is where the real-time form feedback feature is located. In the exercises tab there are lots of different exercises which you can click on individually. Each exercise, has information on the primary and secondary muscles targeted, a small description, and a button to open up the camera and make the magic happen. Once you click on the button, you are asked to allow the app to use your devices camera. Once you give it permission, the front camera opens and you are given directions for how to use the real-time form feedback feature. You can then follow the prompts and suggestions to improve your technique and achieve better results from your workouts.
